Keisha S Perkins is a self-taught freelance designer and developer from Baton Rouge, Louisiana. The goal of her designs is to allow form and function to meet client needs. KayEssPea Design, LLC, a Baton Rouge graphic design company, was formed in 2015. KayEssPea Design’s core competency is branding for small businesses in the state of Louisiana.
